# 🌵 Hydrating Tips for Dry Skin

Dry skin lacks oil and moisture, which can make it feel tight or flaky. Here is a quick guide to keeping dry skin hydrated, plump, and glowing.

## 💧 Daily Best Practices
* **Use a Gentle Cleanser:** Avoid harsh foaming cleansers that strip natural oils. Opt for milk, cream, or oil-based cleansers.
* **Apply Products on Damp Skin:** Pat your face lightly after washing, and apply your toner or moisturizer while your skin is still slightly damp to lock in maximum hydration.
* **Look for Hydrating Ingredients:** Choose products containing **Hyaluronic Acid**, **Glycerin**, **Ceramides**, and **Squalane**.
* **Layer Your Moisture:** Use a hydrating toner, follow with a nourishing serum, and seal it all in with a rich, thick moisturizer.

## 🚫 Things to Avoid
* **Hot Water:** Wash your face with lukewarm water instead, as hot water strips away essential skin oils.
* **Over-Exfoliating:** Limit physical scrubs or chemical exfoliants (like AHAs/BHAs) to just once a week.
