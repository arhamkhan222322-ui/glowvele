# 🌟 Ultimate Korean Skincare Routine Guide

Korean skincare (K-Beauty) is all about hydration, protection, and achieving that healthy "glass skin" glow. Here is a simple daily routine you can follow.

## 🧴 The Day Routine (Steps)
1. **Cleanse:** Wash with water or a gentle water-based cleanser.
2. **Toner:** Pat a hydrating toner into the skin to balance pH.
3. **Essence / Serum:** Apply a targeted serum (like Vitamin C for brightening).
4. **Moisturizer:** Lock in the hydration with a lightweight moisturizer.
5. **Sunscreen (Crucial!):** Apply SPF 50+ to protect your skin from UV rays.

## 🌙 The Night Routine (Steps)
1. **Double Cleanse:** Use an oil-based cleanser first to remove makeup/sebum, followed by a water-based foaming cleanser.
2. **Toner & Essence:** Hydrate deeply.
3. **Ampoule / Serum:** Use repair serums (like Hyaluronic Acid or Retinol).
4. **Eye Cream:** Gently tap around the eye area.
